## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🩺 NebulaFlow — AI Medical Billing Platform
> AI-powered OHIP billing optimization and medical scribe for Ontario physicians. Extracts billing codes from clinical notes, validates against Ontario's Schedule of Benefits, and surfaces missed revenue.

`Next.js` `Supabase` `OpenAI Whisper` `n8n` `OHIP` `PHIPA`

---

### 💬 WhatsApp AI Outreach System
> End-to-end AI outreach automation built for a Dubai real estate client. Handles outbound sequences (text, PDF, business card) and AI-powered inbound replies across a 40,000-row property owner dataset.

`n8n` `Meta WhatsApp Business API` `Google Gemini` `Supabase` `PostgreSQL`

---

### 🐟 National Bait Inc. — Business Website
> Professional business website for a fishing bait distributor. Includes AODA-compliant PDF viewer, EmailJS contact forms, and full DNS/deployment setup.

`React` `TypeScript` `Tailwind CSS` `Netlify` `Cloudflare DNS` `EmailJS`

---

### 🏥 Medical Code Extractor
> Local AI pipeline that reads clinical notes and extracts validated ICD-10 and CPT billing codes, producing structured HTML reports — all running offline on an RTX 3060.

`n8n` `Ollama` `llama3.2` `ICD-10` `CPT` `Local LLM`

---

### 🎮 Jet Bomb Duels — Roblox Game
> Multiplayer arena PvP game featuring jetpack mechanics, bomb combat, cosmetic skin systems, a virtual currency (Fuze), DataStore persistence, and a full in-game shop and inventory UI.

`Roblox Studio` `Luau` `DataStore` `RemoteEvents` `GUI Scripting`
